# Brand Decision

Final public name: `Alesvia`

Backup name: `Selavika`

Reasoning:
- `Alesvia` was the strongest blend of pronounceability, institutional tone, and distinctiveness in a balanced Czech/English reading.
- A quick exact-match web check did not surface an obvious organization using `Alesvia`; the visible hit was a personal name rather than a public brand.
- `alesvia.org` and `alesvia.com` did not resolve during the DNS check run in this workspace.
- `Selavika` remains the backup because it is still pronounceable in both languages and its `.org` and `.com` domains also did not resolve in the same DNS check.

Shortlist used for the decision:

| Name | Pronounceability | Distinctiveness | Institutional tone | Mission fit | Quick screen note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Alesvia | 5/5 | 4/5 | 4/5 | 4/5 | Cleanest overall result |
| Selavika | 4/5 | 4/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | Viable backup |
| Alenvia | 4/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | `.com` resolved, deprioritized |
| Avenika | 4/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | Weaker institutional feel |
| Lusevia | 4/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | `.com` resolved, deprioritized |
| Consikia | 3/5 | 4/5 | 3/5 | 3/5 | Distinctive but weaker on first-hear pronunciation |
