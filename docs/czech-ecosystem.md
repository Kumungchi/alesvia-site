## Universities & Research

### Czech Technical University (CTU) — Artificial Intelligence Center (AIC)
- **What:** Leading Czech AI research lab within CTU's Faculty of Electrical Engineering
- **Focus:** Machine learning, computer vision, robotics, game theory
- **Relevance:** Academic partnership for Alesvia Proof (evidence-based AI literacy), research collaboration for Mind and Watch

### Charles University — OpenEuroLLM Project
- **What:** €34M EU-funded project to build sovereign European large language models
- **Relevance:** Major investment in European AI sovereignty; natural partner for Policy Lab (EU AI Act compliance) and Watch (monitoring European AI development)

### CEDMO 2.0 (Central European Digital Media Observatory)
- **What:** CZK 175M grant for combating disinformation using AI
- **Partners:** Masaryk University, Palacký University, Demagog.cz
- **Relevance:** Directly aligned with Alesvia Watch and Proof; disinformation detection intersects with digital literacy and AI monitoring

### Masaryk University (Brno)
- **What:** Strong informatics and social science faculties
- **Relevance:** CEDMO partner, potential academic collaborator for Mind (AI + psychology research)

### Palacký University (Olomouc)
- **What:** CEDMO partner, media studies and digital humanities
- **Relevance:** Regional university with aligned interests in media literacy

## State Programs & Policy

### National AI Strategy 2030
- **What:** Czech government's roadmap for AI development and governance
- **Relevance:** Sets the policy context Alesvia Policy Lab operates within; defines government priorities for AI investment

### TWIST Programme
- **Budget:** CZK 5 billion (~€200M)
- **What:** Technology and Innovation Support Programme managed by Czech Technology Agency (TA ČR)
- **Relevance:** Major potential funding source for Alesvia initiatives, especially Proof (education) and Watch (research)

### CzechInvest AI Hub
- **What:** State investment agency initiative connecting AI companies with resources, clients, and international markets
- **Relevance:** Partnership channel for Alesvia Advisory (connecting ethical AI startups with support)

### Regulatory Sandbox
- **What:** Czech government piloting AI regulatory sandbox for testing AI applications in controlled environments
- **Relevance:** Alesvia Compass could provide ethical evaluation framework within sandbox testing

### EU AI Act Implementation
- **Budget:** CZK 232M allocated by Czech government for implementation
- **What:** National implementation of EU AI Act compliance requirements
- **Relevance:** Core operational context for Policy Lab; Compass can offer compliance guidance

## Key Takeaways

1. **The Czech AI ecosystem is small but growing fast** — 70+ companies, $248M raised, concentrated in Prague with academic strength in Brno and Olomouc
2. **Government is actively investing** — CZK 5B TWIST programme + CZK 232M for EU AI Act implementation creates funding opportunities
3. **Disinformation/media literacy is a strong local theme** — CEDMO 2.0 with CZK 175M is the clearest alignment with Alesvia's mission
4. **prg.ai is the ecosystem connector** — membership should be a priority for visibility and partnerships
5. **European sovereignty is emerging** — OpenEuroLLM (€34M) signals growing interest in independent European AI development
6. **Regulatory sandbox is an opportunity** — Alesvia Compass could position as the ethical evaluation partner within the sandbox framework
